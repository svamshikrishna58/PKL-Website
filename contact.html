FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

🎟️
How do I buy tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online through our official website, on the BookMyShow platform, or at the venue box office on match day. Season passes are also available for select venues.

📺
Where can I watch PKL?

Pro Kabaddi League Season 11 is broadcast live on Star Sports channels. You can also stream all matches live on Disney+ Hotstar across India and select international markets.

🤝
How do I partner with PKL?

We offer various partnership opportunities including title sponsorships, associate sponsorships, broadcast partners, and digital partnerships. Email partnerships@prokabaddi.com for our media kit.

⚡
How are players selected?

Players are acquired through the annual PKL Auction where all 12 franchises bid on domestic and international players. Each team has a salary cap of ₹4 Crore per season.
